Welcome!

This was the first dynamic website made by Brandon Rehayem. It was built with a humble flask architecture and was truly a labor of love. Though I now have experience with using more complex technologies, this site holds a special place for me as a critical benchmark in my understanding of web development.

As R. Lee Ermey put it: "There are many like it, but this one is mine."

You can navigate the website by clicking the items listed at the top. If you're up to it, why not leave a short poem or try to get a highscore in asteroids? My portfolio is also available. Enjoy your time here.
